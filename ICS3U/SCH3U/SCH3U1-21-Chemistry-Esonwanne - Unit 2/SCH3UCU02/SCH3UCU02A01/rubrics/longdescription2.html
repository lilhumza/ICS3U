Chemical Processes and Reactions and Effects on Human Health and Environment

Category: Knowledge and Understanding, identifies the relevant chemical reactions, reactant, products, and effects on human health and environment.
SI1.07
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59). Demonstrates limited knowledge of the relevant chemical reactions, reactant, products, and effects on human health and environment.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Demonstrates some knowledge of relevant chemical reactions, reactant, products, and effects on human health and environment.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Demonstrates considerable knowledge of the relevant chemical reactions, reactant, products, and effects on human health and environment.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Demonstrates thorough knowledge of the chemical reactions, reactant, products, and effects on human health and environment.

Category: Communication, communication of information, analysis and proposal. SI1.11
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with limited clarity and purpose.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with moderate clarity and purpose.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with considerable clarity and precision.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with a high degree of clarity and precision.

Category: Application, information is analyzed and clearly supports the clearly stated position.CR1.01
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Position, support and analysis are of limited effectiveness.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Position, support and analysis are of moderate effectiveness
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Position, support and analysis are of considerable effectiveness.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Position, support and analysis are of a high degree of effectiveness.

Note: A student whose achievement is below Level 1 (50%) has not met the expectations for this assignment or activity.
