Unit 2: Chemical Reactions

Activity 1: Reactions, Society and the Environment

Long Description of Rubric

Chemical Reactions and Their Effectiveness

Category: Knowledge and Understanding, identifies the purpose and pertinent chemical reactions and the effects they have on the environment and humans. SI1.07
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59). Demonstrates limited knowledge of the purpose and relevant chemical reaction(s) and effects they have on the environment and humans.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Demonstrates some knowledge of the purpose and relevent chemical reaction(s) and effects they have on the environment and humans.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Demonstrates considerable knowledge of  the purpose and relevant chemical reaction(s) and effects they have on the environment and humans.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%).-demonstrates thorough knowledge of  the purpose and relevant chemical reaction(s) and effects they have on the environment and humans.

Category: Communication, communication of information, analysis and proposal. SI1.11
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with limited clarity and purpose.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with moderate clarity and purpose.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with considerable clarity and precision.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with a high degree of clarity and precision.

Category: Application, analyzes the effects of the chemical reactions and takes a stand on the issues surrounding the subject. MC1.01
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Analyzes the effects of the chemical reactions and takes a stand on the issues surrounding the subject with limited effectiveness
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Analyzes the effects of the chemical reactions and takes a stand on the issues surrounding the subject with moderate effectiveness
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Analyzes the environmental impact with considerable effectiveness
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Analyzes the effects of the chemical reactions and takes a stand on the issues surrounding the subject with a high degree of effectiveness.

Note: A student whose achievement is below Level 1 (50%) has not met the expectations for this assignment or activity.
