Content

Required Reading

Complete the following questions before, during and after you read the article There’s Something Smelly About This.

Questions

Answer the following questions.

Note:  Some information will be found in the article and other information will need to be found elsewhere. You will have to do a little bit of research to complement the information found in the article.

  1. How does the author view a “cow field” and a “garbage dump”?
    Do you feel the same way?  Why?
  2. What are the sources of methane and what are the potential positive effects on the sustainability of the environment?  Did you know about this potential?
  3. What is “anaerobic digestion”?  And what does it involve?  Write the chemical word equation for anaerobic digestion. What are the products of this chemical reaction and what health and safety concerns are connected with the products of this reaction?  What benefits does the author share about this process?  Do you foresee any problems concerning anaerobic digestion, for people who live in a neighbourhood where this technology might be located? 
  4. Would you promote or discourage biogas technology based on the anaerobic reaction?  Provide reasons for your decision.

Journal

Keep the answers to the questions in your journal for future reference. Your teacher may ask you to hand in your journal entries later in the course.
