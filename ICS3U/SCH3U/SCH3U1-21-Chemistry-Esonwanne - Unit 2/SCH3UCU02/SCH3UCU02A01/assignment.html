Assignment

Assignment 1: Chemical Reactions and Their Effectiveness

Complete the following assignment and submit your work to the dropbox.

Before you upload your file, ensure that your name appears on the top of every page of your document.

For this assignment, your teacher will assign you a partner or assign you to a group.

As a team, assess the effectiveness of some applications of chemical reactions that are used to address social and environmental needs, issues or problems. For example, fluoridation of drinking water in Canada and the United States for dental hygiene has become very controversial. Use such ideas or social issues in your presentation.

Your report should be in the form of a presentation using Corel Presentation, PowerPoint or similar presentation software approved by your teacher.

Suggested Framework/Issues:

Framework:

  1. Include an introduction and a conclusion in your presentation;.
  2. Use appropriate diagrams or videos.
  3. Ensure you address environmental degradation.

Issues and questions:

  1. How can chemical reactions turn a toxic chemical into one that is less toxic or non-toxic?
  2. How are chemical reactions used to remediate areas affected by industrial accidents?
  3. What are risks and benefits of industrial scrubbers' reactions to reduce pollution?

Assignment 2: Chemical Reactions in Industry

Complete the following assignment and submit your work to the dropbox.

Before you upload your file, ensure that your name appears on the top of every page of your document.

Select a career or sector in an industry that involves the use of chemical reactions. This may include extracting, purifying or synthesizing industrial chemicals. These chemical reactions may include gasses, organic and inorganic chemicals, or synthetic materials used in hospitals, industries, or your home. Research the processes involved in that industry. Analyze the risks and benefits of the chemical processes that affect both the environment and people's health.

Present your findings and analysis in poster form.
