Overview

In addition to a chemical’s impact on the environment, its direct effect on human health is an important issue for the sustainability of human life. The production and use of chemicals reaches far and wide in society.

In this activity you will evaluate the risks and benefits of using chemicals in different environments. You will work cooperatively in a small group, or as an individual, to select the application of a chemical reaction and assess its usefulness and effects on humans and the environment. A second assignment asks you to research an industrial process involving chemical reactions and the effects of that reaction on the environment.
