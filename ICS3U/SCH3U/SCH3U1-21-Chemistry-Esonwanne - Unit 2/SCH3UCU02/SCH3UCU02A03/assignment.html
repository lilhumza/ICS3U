Lab

The purpose of this assignment is to investigate:

  • the neutralization of an acid-base reaction; and
  • the respective reactions between a metal oxide, non-metal oxide and water.

Part A Neutralization of an Acid-Base Reaction

A small beaker of hydrochloric acid is tested with a strip of universal indicator paper before a solution of sodium hydroxide was added drop wise to the acid. The starting pH of the hydrochloric acid was 1. At various points between the additions of drops, clean strips of the universal indicator paper were dipped into the solution and the colours were recorded and matched to a colour scheme connected to a pH value. Answer the following questions:

  1. Describe the trend in the changes in pH values as sodium hydroxide was being added to the hydrochloric acid.

  2. What are the two products of a neutralization reaction between the hydrochloric acid and sodium hydroxide?

  3. For the reaction between sodium hydroxide and hydrochloric acid write:
    1. A word chemical equation
    2. A skeletal chemical equation
    3. balanced chemical equation.

Extension Questions:
