Expectations

Overall Expectations:

SIV.01  demonstrate scientific investigation skills (related to both inquiry and research) in the four areas of skills (initiating and planning, performing and recording, analysing and interpreting, and communicating);
CRV.02 investigate different types of chemical reactions.

Specific Expectations:

SI1.12 use appropriate numeric, symbolic, and graphic modes of representation, and appropriate units of measurement (e.g., SI and imperial units);
CR2.01 use appropriate terminology related to chemical reactions, including, but not limited to:  neutralization, precipitate, acidic, and base;
CR2.02 write balanced chemical equations to represent synthesis, decomposition, single displacement, double displacement, and combustion reactions, using IUPAC nomenclature system.

Catholic Graduate Expectations:

CGE2b reads, understands and uses written materials effectively;
CGE2d writes and speaks fluently one or both of Canada’s official languages.
