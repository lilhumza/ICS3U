Overview

In this activity, you will focus on the recognition and an understanding of the basic components of a chemical equation that represents a chemical reaction consisting of various types of matter. You will also explore the process involved in balancing a chemical equation and examine examples that illustrate this process. You will have the opportunity to apply what you’ve learned in the assignment section.
