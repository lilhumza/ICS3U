Overview

In today’s world, quantitative relationships in chemical reactions are very important. In this activity you will be provided with an opportunity to investigate and learn a fundamental quantitative concept of a chemical reaction. You will also explore and learn that the mass relationships between reactants and products are an integral part of chemistry. The area of chemistry dealing with mass relationships between reactants and products in a chemical reaction is called stoichiometry.  Opportunities are provided to view video clips of chemical reactions and a simulation will allow you to develop an understanding of the concept of stoichiometry.
