Overview

This activity will provide you with a starting point for Unit 3: Quantities in Chemical Reactions. You will explore the applications and relevance of calculations involving chemicals in the context of science, technology, society and the environment. Calculations are a vital part of chemistry and are used in chemistry-related situations. You will have the chance to examine and practice significant figures and in the assignment section you will have the opportunity to explore careers in chemistry.
