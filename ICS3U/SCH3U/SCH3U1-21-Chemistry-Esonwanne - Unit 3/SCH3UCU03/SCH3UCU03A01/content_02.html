Calculations and Significant Figures:

When adding or subtracting numbers, always check to find the number that is the least precise (digits to the right of the decimal) and the answer will be rounded off to the digit of least precision. 

Example

63.29 + 1.3 - 0.0058  =  64.5842 (calculator answer)

(Your answer is 64.6 because 1.3 is the least precise number.)

When multiplying or dividing numbers, round off the answer to the number of significant figures matching the least number of significant figures of the numbers that you were multiplying and/or dividing.

Example

7891 ÷  471 = 16.7537155 (calculator answer)

(Your answer is 16.8 because 471 has three significant figures.)
