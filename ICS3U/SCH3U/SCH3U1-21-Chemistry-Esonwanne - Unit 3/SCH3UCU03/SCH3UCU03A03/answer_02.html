2. Write the chemical word equation for the combustion of magnesium and oxygen gas and then write the balanced chemical equation.

Chemical Word Equation is

magnesium  +  oxygen gas        magnesium oxide

Balanced chemical equation is

2Mg(s)   +    O2(g)    2MgO(s)
