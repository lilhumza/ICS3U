Assignment

Assignment 1

Complete the following assignment and submit your work to the dropbox.

Before you upload your file, ensure that your name appears on the top of every page of your document.

1. How many grains of sand are there in 0.03 moles?
(Thinking marks = 1)

2. Calculate the number of atoms in each of the following quantities:
(Thinking marks = 2 x 1 = 2)

a) 3.5 moles of sodium atoms;
b) 0.5 moles of helium atom.

3. Calculate the number of moles of atoms in each of the following quantities:
(Thinking marks = 2 x 1 = 2)

a)2.7 x 1024 atoms of neon;
b)? 3.2 x 1012 atoms of hydrogen.

4. Calculate the molar mass of the following compounds:
(Thinking marks = 4 x 2 = 8)

a) CO2
b) HNO3
c) Al(OH)3
d) SiCl4

5. Calculate the number of molecules of:
(Thinking marks = 2 x 1 = 2)

a) 3.5 moles of water.
b) 100 g of ozone, O3.

6. Calculate the mass, in grams, of each of the following:

a) 5 moles of nitrogen dioxide;
b) 1.8 x 1020 atoms of iron;
c) 7.5 x 1025 molecules of carbon monoxide
(Thinking marks = 3 x 1 = 3)

7. Calculate the number of moles of the following:
(Thinking marks = 3 x 2 = 6)

a) 250 g of water;
b) 50 g of sodium hydroxide, NaOH.
c) 3.5 x 1024 molecules of methane, CH4

8. Calculate the number of atoms of carbon in each of the following:
(Thinking marks = 2 x 2 = 4)

a) 3.8 moles of methane, CH4
b) 100 g of ethane, C2H6.

9. Calculate the mass of chlorine in 2 moles of carbon tetrachloride, CCl4.
(Thinking marks = 1 x 2 = 2)

Total Thinking marks = 30; Total communication marks = 2 x 9 = 18
