Overview

In this activity, you will explore information and sample calculations concerning the concept of the mole and its importance in the foundation of chemical principles. The role of the mole will guide your calculations concerning the mass of elements, compounds and other related matter. You will also be provided with examples using the mole in calculating numbers of atoms and molecules and vice versa. Opportunities for self assessment are interspersed throughout the content section and an assignment and a quiz are the two instruments used to evaluate your mastery of the mole concept.
