Activity 3: Gas Everywhere

Answer

  1. In a chemical reaction where hydrogen gas is collected over water at a temperature of 25°C, the total pressure of the water vapour and hydrogen gas is 740 mmHg. The vapour pressure of water vapour at 25°C is 23.76 mmHg.
    Calculate the pressure of the oxygen gas.

Pressure of the oxygen gas = Ptotal - Pwater vapour
Pressure of the oxygen gas = 740mmHg - 23.76mmHg
Pressure of the oxygen gas = 716mmHg
