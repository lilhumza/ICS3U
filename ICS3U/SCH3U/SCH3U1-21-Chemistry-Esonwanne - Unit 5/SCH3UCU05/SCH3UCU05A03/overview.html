Overview

This activity continues your exploration of the gas laws into the area of stoichiometry. You will examine reactions involving gases and their quantities and relate them to the gas laws. It is critical in this activity that you follow all steps in the process and make accurate calculations. In the first assignment you will investigate the collection of hydrogen gas over water and in the second assignment you will apply stoichiometry to the chemistry of airbag.
