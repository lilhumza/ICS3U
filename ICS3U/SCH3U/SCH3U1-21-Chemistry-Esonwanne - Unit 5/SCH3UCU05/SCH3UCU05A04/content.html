Content

Review

The following main areas of the unit should be reviewed before taking the unit test:

  • knowledge of the terms:  standard temperature and pressure, molar volume, ideal gas, combined gas laws, Dalton’s law of partial pressures, Avogadro’s law, Boyle’s law, Charles’ law, Gay-Lussac’s law, ideal gas law, ideal gas, and real gas;
  • calculations involving: pressures, temperatures, moles and volumes of gases;
  • characteristics of states of matter and their unique differences especially the gaseous phase.

In preparation for the unit test, try this review test.

Show all your work in the solutions to the numerical problems and rules of significant figures when stating the final answer.
