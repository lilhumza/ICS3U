Overview

In this activity, you will be introduced to the physical characteristics that distinguish the states of matter with a focus on the gaseous state. You will also explore the unique properties of gases and the quantitative nature of these relationships. You will have the opportunity to explore multimedia examples of these concepts and to self assess your understanding of the material presented in the unit. Finally, you will examine Boyle’s and Charles’ law and illustrate the quantitative relationships in these two respective laws.
