Overview

The sustainability of the environment is very dependent on air quality. In this activity, you will explore the effects of industrial and human activities on air quality. You will also examine government initiatives and the application of the Air Quality Health Index in a chosen region of the province in the context of improving air quality. An opportunity to research and present Canadian scientists’ contributions is presented in your last assignment.
