Graphing Periodic Trends

Complete the following assignment and submit your work to the dropbox.


Try the activity titled Graphing Periodic Trends.

Part A: You have been asked to summarize the periodic trends in a group and in a period for the following properties:

  • atomic radius;
  • ionization energy; and
  • electronegativity.

Using the terms increasing or decreasing, fill in the blanks for each of the following statements which correctly reflects the general trend of the respective property across a period and in a group/family using :

  1. Atomic radii _______________ going left to right in a period across the Periodic Table.

  2. Atomic radii _______________ going top to bottom in a group/family in the Periodic Table.

  3. Ionization energies _______________ going left to right in a period across the Periodic Table.

  4. Ionization energies _______________ going top to bottom in a group/family in the Periodic Table.

  5. Electronegativities _______________ going left to right in a period across the Periodic Table.

  6. Electronegativities _______________ going top to bottom in a group/family in the Periodic Table.

Part B: Answer the following questions using proper grammar and full sentences:

  1. Summarize the metallic and non-metallic characteristics of the periodic properties.
  1. Explain the atomic radius trend in a group and in a period.
  1. Explain the relationship between atomic radius and ionization energy trends in a group and in a period.
  1. Compare the relative energies between a 2s orbital and a 4s orbital. What clues are there to verify this comparison?
  1. Suggest a reason why there is not a significant change in ionization energies when electrons are removed from the same electron's shell.
  1. Which group would not have data concerning electronegativity? In terms of their electrons and their orbitals, suggest a reason.
