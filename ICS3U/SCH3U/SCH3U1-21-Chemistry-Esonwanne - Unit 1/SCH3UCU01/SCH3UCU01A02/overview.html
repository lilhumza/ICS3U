Overview

Chemical substances have a tremendous impact on the environment. They also have a direct impact on human health and subsequently, on the sustainability of human life. The production and use of chemicals reaches far and wide in society.  In this activity, you will explore the risks and benefits associated with using chemicals.  You will also be introduced to the important roles played by chemists and other scientists working in related careers in specific sectors of the chemical industry.  You will work cooperatively in small groups, or individually, to select a commonly used chemical substance or product, and evaluate its risks and benefits to human health.
