Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). -expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with limited clarity and purpose
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with moderate clarity and purpose

Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). -analyzes the environmental impact with moderate effectiveness
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). -analyzes the environmental impact with considerable effectiveness
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). -analyzes the environmental impacts with a high degree of effectiveness

Note: A student whose achievement is below Level 1 (50%) has not met the expectations for this assignment.
