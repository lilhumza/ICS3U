Chemicals Are In the House

Your place of residence, be it a house, apartment or condominium, offers you a place to be with family and friends to pray, sleep, eat, do homework and relax. It is also a place that you should feel safe in. You can make it even safer by ensuring that you are aware of the chemicals that could be found in your home. Some chemical based items in the house are there because they serve a particular purpose.

For this assignment you will produce a pamphlet focusing on a commonly used chemical substance. You will research its risks and benefits to human health and the environment and propose alternatives to this chemical or ways in which to minimize its effects on human health and the environment.

Creating your pamphlet:

    1. Research your information by finding it in other sources- e.g., Internet, books, magazines, journals.
    2. Organize and classify your information- e.g., charts, graphic organizers.
    3. Write an introduction to your topic.
    4. Make sure that there is a sequence and the order of information and ideas that are presented in the pamphlet.
    5. Decide how you are going to present the information in your pamphlet.
    6. Write a conclusion that summarizes the main topics, ideas, suggestion.
    7. Credit to your sources in a properly written bibliography.

Your pamphlet should include:

    1. The chemical clearly identified.
    2. An introduction and conclusion.
    3. Risks and benefits to human health and the environment.
    4. Alternatives to this chemical or methods of reducing the risks of this chemical.
    5. A separate, properly written bibliography. Please check with your teacher regarding the format that he or she prefers.
