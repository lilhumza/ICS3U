Overview

In this activity you will examine chemical bonds, Lewis diagrams and the chemical properties of ionic and molecular compounds. You will be given the opportunity to draw Lewis diagrams and assess your own understanding of them in a quiz. A group assignment will allow you to compare the physical properties of simple binary ionic and molecular compounds.
