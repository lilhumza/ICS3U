Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Demonstrates knowledge of the chemicals found in antifreeze and their relevant chemical and physical properties and identifies benefits for humans and risks to humans and environment.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Demonstrates considerable knowledge of the chemicals found in antifreeze and their relevant chemical and physical properties and identifies benefits for humans and risks to humans and environment
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Demonstrates thorough knowledge of the chemicals found in antifreeze and their relevant chemical and physical properties and identifies benefits for humans and risks to humans and environment.

Category: Communication, communication of information, analysis and proposal. SI1.11
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with limited clarity and purpose
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with moderate clarity and purpose
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with considerable clarity and precision
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Expresses and organizes information, analysis and ideas with considerable clarity and precision

Note: A student whose achievement is below Level 1 (50%) has not met the expectations for this assignment.
