Assignment 1

Complete the following assignment and submit your work to the dropbox.

Investigating Antifreeze

Antifreeze is a common chemical product that helps to keep engines cool. Research antifreeze and answer the following questions in a report:

  1. Identify the chemicals found in antifreeze and the reasons they are part of the antifreeze product.
  2. What are the formulas for these chemicals?
  3. State the chemical and physical properties of these substances.
  4. What are the benefits of antifreeze?
  5. What are the risks to the environment with antifreeze?
  6. Are there opportunities to lessen its effects on the environment?
  7. Are there any alternatives?

Assignment 2

Complete the following assignment and submit your work to the dropbox.

Before you upload your file, ensure your names appear on the top of every page of your document.

Group Work

In this assignment, your teacher will assign you to a research team. Your group can meet in the class chat room, use the paging tool or you may simply prefer to communicate via email. Your teacher may also provide other meeting place alternatives.

As a team you will research the properties of a potentially harmful chemical substance (e.g. lead paint, fertilizer, pesticide, herbicide, a household cleaning product). Your first job is to decide, as a team, which chemical you will research and submit your choice to your teacher for approval.

You will prepare and submit a report that analyses how that substance affects the environment and propose ways to lessen the harmful effects of this substance on the environment. You will present your findings, analysis and proposal in the form of a presentation using Microsoft PowerPoint, Corel Presentation or similar presentation software.
