Overview

This activity provides you with an introduction to the classification of matter and its chemical and physical properties.  You will revisit your understanding of the classification of matter, and the chemical and physical properties of matter. There are two assignments connected to this activity.  In the first assignment, you will produce a report on antifreeze.  You will examine its effects on human health and the environment, and propose alternatives to its use and produce a report.  In the second assignment, you will work cooperatively with a small group and produce a presentation on a potentially harmful chemical substance.  You will analyze your findings and propose alternatives to the substance under study.
