Overview

Nomenclature in chemistry provides an international thread that helps to bind chemical information and procedures relying on the transfer of this information.  Common everyday chemical products will be known for their brand name, but for scientists and chemists, the internationally recognized names and symbols of the chemicals are the same regardless of what language they speak.  Recognition and the proper application of chemical names and formulas are vital in communication within and between the scientific communities and non-scientific communities.

In this activity, you will explore the International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry (IUPAC) system of naming and writing formulas of inorganic compounds.
