Chart 3

Section B     
For the simple binary ionic compounds, BeS, CsC1 and CaO provide the:

  • Name and formula;
  • Lewis dot diagram;
  • Ball and stick model of crystal lattice;
  • Labelled 3-D drawing of the crystal lattice showing the locations of the ions at the lattice points

Please note that the crystal lattice for BeS and CaO is a face-centred cube and for CsC1 it is a simple cube.
