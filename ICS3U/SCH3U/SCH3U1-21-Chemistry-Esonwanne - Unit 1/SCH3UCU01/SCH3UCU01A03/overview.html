Overview

This activity will introduce you to some of the terms and vocabulary that are critical to your understanding of chemistry: the atom, isotopic symbols, isotopes, radioisotopes, relative atomic mass, atomic orbital, quantum numbers, and periodic properties of element.  You will be given opportunities to self assess your understanding of these terms and vocabulary.
