Answer

1. In the long term, what potential water table problem could there be in the septic tank scenario?

Over a long period of time, the build up of contaminants in the soil could reach significant levels that would be harmful to the immediate environment.  Nature acts as a filter, but undue stress would counteract the natural filtering process, resulting in a contaminated water table.
