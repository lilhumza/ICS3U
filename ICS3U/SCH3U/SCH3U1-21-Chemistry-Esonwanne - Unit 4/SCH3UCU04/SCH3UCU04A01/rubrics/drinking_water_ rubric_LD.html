SCH3U: Chemistry, Grade 11, University Preparation

Unit 4: Solutions and Solubility

Activity 1: Water Everywhere

Long Description of Rubric


Water Contaminant Rubric.

Category: Knowledge and Understanding, knowledge of the relevant information related to examining the economic, social, environmental and public health factors of municipal drinking water and bottled water. SI1.01
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Demonstrates limited knowledge of the relevant information related to examining the economic, social, environmental and public health factors of municipal drinking water and bottled water.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Demonstrates some knowledge of the relevant information related to examining the economic, social, environmental and public health factors of municipal drinking water and bottled water.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Demonstrates considerable knowledge of the relevant information related to examining the economic, social, environmental and public health factors of municipal drinking water and bottled water.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Demonstrates thorough knowledge of the relevant information related to examining the economic, social, environmental and public health factors of municipal drinking water and bottled water.

Category: Thinking and Investigation, use of processing skills toward gathering evidence and data. SI1.07
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Uses limited processing skills in gathering evidence and data.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Uses some processing skills in gathering evidence and data.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%).  Uses considerable processing skills in gathering evidence and data.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Uses thorough processing skills in gathering evidence and data.

Category: Communication, expression and organization of information and explanation. SI1.11
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Expresses and organizes information and analysis with limited degree of clarity and focus.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Expresses and organizes information and analysis with some degree of clarity and focus.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Expresses and organizes information and analysis with considerable degree of clarity and focus.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Expresses and organizes information and analysis with a high degree of clarity and focus.

Category: Application, assesses and analyzes the impacts on the economic, social, environmental, and public health areas connected to drinking water from water purification plants and the use of plastic bottles for water. SI1.02
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Assesses and analyzes the impacts, with limited efficiency, on the economic, social, environmental, and public health areas linked to drinking water from a municipal water treatment plant and bottled water.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Assesses and analyzes the impacts, with some efficiency, on the economic, social, environmental, and public health areas linked to drinking water from a municipal water treatment plant and bottled water.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Assesses and analyzes the impacts, with considerable efficiency, on the economic, social, environmental, and public health areas linked to drinking water from a municipal water treatment plant and bottled water.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Assesses and analyzes the impacts, with thorough efficiency, on the economic, social, environmental, and public health areas linked to drinking water from a municipal water treatment plant and bottled water.

Note: A student whose achievement is below Level 1 (50%) has not met the expectations for this assignment or activity.
