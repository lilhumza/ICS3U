SCH3U: Chemistry, Grade 11, University Preparation

Unit 4: Solutions and Solubility

Activity 1: Water Everywhere

Long Description of Rubric


Water Contaminant Rubric.

Category: Knowledge and Understanding, knowledge of water pollutant, its origins and cumulative effects on the environment. SI1.07
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Demonstrates limited knowledge of water pollutant, its origins and cumulative effects on the environment.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Demonstrates some knowledge of water pollutant, its origins and cumulative effects on the environment.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Demonstrates considerable knowledge of water pollutant, its origins and cumulative effects on the environment.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Demonstrates thorough knowledge of water pollutant, its origins and cumulative effects on the environment.

Category: Thinking and Investigation, use of analytical thinking skills and processes in the formulation of explaining the affects of a pollutant on water quality. SS1.01
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Uses limited analytical thinking skills and processes in the formulation of explaining the effects of a pollutant on water quality.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Uses somee analytical thinking skills and processes in the formulation of explaining the effects of a pollutant on water quality.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%).  Uses considerable analytical thinking skills and processes in the formulation of explaining the effects of a pollutant  on water quality.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Uses thorough analytical thinking skills and processes in the formulation of explaining the effects of a pollutant  on water quality with a high degree of effectiveness.

Category: Communication, expression and organization of information and explanation. SI1.1
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Expresses and organizes information and explanation with limited degree of clarity and focus.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Expresses and organizes information and explanation with some degree of clarity and focus.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Expresses and organizes information and explanation with considerable degree of clarity and focus.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Expresses and organizes information and explanation with a high degree of clarity and focus.

Category: Application, makes connections between science, technology, society and the environment in the explanation of how a water pollutant affects water quality. SI1.10
Criteria: Level 1, (50-59%). Makes connections between science, technology, society and the environment in the explanation of how a water pollutant affects water quality with limited efficiency.
Criteria: Level 2, (60-69%). Makes connections between science, technology, society and the environment in the explanation of how a water pollutant affects water quality with some efficiency.
Criteria: Level 3, (70-79%). Makes connections between science, technology, society and the environment in the explanation of how a water pollutant affects water quality with considerable efficiency.
Criteria: Level 4, (80-100%). Makes connections between science, technology, society and the environment in the explanation of how a water pollutant affects water quality with thorough efficiency.

Note: A student whose achievement is below Level 1 (50%) has not met the expectations for this assignment or activity.
