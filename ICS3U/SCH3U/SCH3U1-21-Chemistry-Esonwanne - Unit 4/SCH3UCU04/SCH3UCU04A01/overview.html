Overview

In this activity, you will gain insight into the importance of water in today’s society. Your analytical and research skills will be utilized to assess the human potential for sustaining the aquatic environment and to gauge the necessary steps to ensure the quality of water for drinking purposes and other human activities. You will investigate a contaminant, its source and effects on water quality. In the second assignment, you will be provided with a scenario that will allow you to make informed decisions about the water supply in your hypothetical township.
