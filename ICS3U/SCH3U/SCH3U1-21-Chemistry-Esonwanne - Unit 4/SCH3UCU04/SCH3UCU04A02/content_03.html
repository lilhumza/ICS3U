Content

The presence of minute amounts of substances in solutions calls for concentration units to be presented in a more easily understood mode. Therefore, dilute concentrations of solutions are commonly expressed in parts per million (ppm) or parts per billion (ppb).

Other concentration units equated to ppm are as follows:

1 ppm and 1 ppb
= 1 g/106 mL   = 1 g/109 mL
= 1 g/103 L   = 1 g/106 L
= 1 mg/L   = 1 mg/103 L or 1 μg/L
= 1 mg/kg   = 1 μg/kg
= 1 μg/g   = 10-3 μg/g

To equate ppb and ppm, the equation is ppb = 103 x ppm
