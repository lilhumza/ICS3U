Overview

In this activity you will explore the quantitative aspect of chemical reactions, solubility and the acidic and alkaline nature of solutions. Calculations involving pH, solubility and stoichiometric relationships will be your main focus. You will be evaluated on three assignments. A group assignment related to the lab safety, procedure, and The Workplace Hazardous Materials Information System will remind you of the importance of safety in a laboratory environment. The second and third assignments allow you to investigate pH and titration, respectively.
